Point of view

I care about the parts of backend engineering that usually fail quietly.

Not just controllers and CRUD. The interesting work is what happens after the request leaves the happy path: delayed jobs, external integrations, retries, reconciliation, state transitions, and the investigation trail when something goes wrong.
