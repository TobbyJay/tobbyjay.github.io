SPOTAHUB

Developed by:

Your's truly

Date completed:

MVP(october 2020), v2(still under development)

Technologies Used:

HTML/ CSS/ JS/ BOOTSTRAP/ C#/ .NET CORE MVC/ ENTITY FRAMEWORK CORE/ MSSQL



The Problem: The whole world is going the "Remote" route and as a result, everyone would at some point want to work remotely , and here in Nigeria, there is usually no constant power supply, so everyone would want to find a place to work, i asked a few friends, some on twitter and others close to me, and they said they prefer Hubs and co-working spaces, but the problem is it difficult to locate them, and the truth is, even Google( yes THE Google) does not have all the results for Hubs in Nigeria but i do now thanks to an open source repository i came across on Github(more additions are stil coming up).

The Solution: SPOTAHUB, yes you saw it here first, as the name implies you can come into this application to look for hubs, add hubs, claim your hubs, manage your hub info,review hubs, report and book hubs(coming in v2). ,you can check it out and send your feedback to me, also if you want to collaborate (as i might be making it open source soon), reach out to me here
